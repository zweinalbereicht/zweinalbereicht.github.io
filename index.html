I am currently pursuing a PhD at the Laboratoire de la Physique Théorique de la Matière Condensée in Paris. My field of research is Statistical Physics - or how to describe macroscopically observable quantities, such as temperature or pressure, by analyzing the statistics of all core microscopic objects that compose our world, from atoms to droplets. Although I do spend a lot of time at the lab, I also thrive in outdoorsy activities, in particular mountain climbing ! ⛰️
